SMS Messaging Consent
Please read before completing your membership signup
Who Is Texting You

By providing your phone number and checking the consent box during checkout, you agree to receive automated SMS text messages from Scaled Creators LLC, operating as SC Studio, on behalf of the creator whose Studio you are joining. Messages will be sent from a dedicated 10-digit phone number associated with your creator's Studio.

What Types of Messages You Will Receive

After you opt in, you may receive SMS messages related to your membership, including:

Trial reminders — notifications before your free trial ends, including what you will lose access to if you do not subscribe

Booking confirmations and reminders — confirmation when you book a 1:1 coaching session or register for a live Q&A, and a reminder 30 minutes before it starts

Session cancellations or reschedules — if your creator cancels or reschedules a booked session

New content alerts — optional notifications when your creator publishes a new course or live session you may be interested in

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your activity. During a free trial, you will receive no more than 3 messages over 7 days. After subscribing, you will receive messages only when triggered by a relevant account event, such as a booking or a new session announcement. We do not send promotional blasts or unsolicited marketing messages.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. SC Studio does not charge you for SMS messages, but your mobile carrier may charge standard messaging rates depending on your plan. Contact your carrier if you are unsure whether SMS messages incur a charge on your plan.

How to Opt Out or Get Help

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying to any SMS from us. The following keywords are always available:

STOP Unsubscribes you immediately. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent to your number.
HELP Returns support contact information so you can reach SC Studio directly if you have a question about your messages or membership.
START Re-subscribes you if you have previously opted out and would like to receive messages again.
Your Consent

Consent to receive SMS messages is not required as a condition of purchasing a membership or accessing any content on SC Studio. You may still complete your checkout and use the platform if you do not wish to receive SMS messages — simply leave the consent checkbox unchecked at checkout.

By checking the box labeled "I agree to receive SMS updates from [Creator] Studio" during checkout, you confirm that you have read and understood this disclosure and that you consent to receive the message types described above at the phone number you provided.

Contact

For questions about this policy or to request support, email support@scaledcreators.com or reply HELP to any message you receive from us.
